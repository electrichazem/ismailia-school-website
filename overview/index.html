School and Community

Ismailia STEM High School is a 3-year boarding, public-funded school in Ismailia, Egypt. The school offers a unique system integrating scientific, technological, engineering, and mathematical fields. The school's mission is promoting innovation and creativity to develop the new generations of Egypt through hands-on projects addressing the nation's pressing challenges.

Taught by teachers involved in post-teacher studies, the core skills and values acquired by the students throughout the curriculum emphasize and promote critical thinking and research, problem-solving skills, intellectual curiosity, and social responsibility.

Our Vision

At Ismailia STEM High School, we believe that the school was designed to create "centres of excellence" that embody trans-disciplinary collaboration, real world applications, critical thinking, and a pioneering spirit.

Ultimately, STEM schools support a new vision of Egypt as a country equipped with both the human capital and the institutional resources to compete and excel in the international marketplace.

Our Mission

The mission of Ismailia STEM High School is to provide students a challenging learning environment focused on math, science, and technology, to inspire joy at the prospect of discovery, and to foster a culture of innovation based on ethical behavior and the shared interests of humanity.

Targeted Students

The school targets high-potential students who demonstrate strong academic skills during the preparatory stage and with enthusiasm for Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics, serving as a cultural melting pot that gathers students from all of Egypt's governments, rendering an acceptance rate of about 1%, which is one of the highest in the nation.

Entrance Exam Qualifications

To qualify for the entrance exam to STEM Egypt schools, applicants should meet one of three criteria (Annually updated):

  • Having more than 98% in the 9th Grade Governmental Exams
  • Having a total grade of more than 95% in the 9th Grade Governmental Exams, with a 100% grade in one of three subjects: Math, Science, and English
  • Got a patent before 9th Grade

Once you pass the entrance exams, you are accepted into one of the STEM schools and you might get accepted into Ismailia STEM High School

Accreditation

The school is accredited by the Egyptian Ministry of Education (MOE) and the United States Agency of International Development (USAID) providing a unique system that focuses on scientific, mathematical and technological fields for academically achieving students.

Campus Life

The school building accommodates about 450 students. The school is coeducational, with nearly equal numbers of male and female students, and there is no distinction in any aspect of the educational process, available subjects, or activities.

All students hold Egyptian nationality. With everyone coming from different governments in Egypt, that gives the students a chance to experience the culture and thoughts diversity based on where they came from, whether they were from a big city or living in rural areas. Thus, this makes our students more accepting and understanding of the differences around them.

The school includes 18 classrooms, 6 for each grade. For each classroom, students are classified into groups where each group helps each other in doing school work such as presentations, and also help each other in understanding the curriculum without outside help.

School Residence

The dorm building is uniquely made to fit a number of 450 students, some teachers, and workers. The building is split into two wings, one for boys residence and the other for girls, each wing contains 4 study rooms and each one contains a balcony.

The building includes a dining hall that offers nutritious meals to students for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. All study rooms are equipped with Wi-Fi that could only be used by laptops for educational purposes.
