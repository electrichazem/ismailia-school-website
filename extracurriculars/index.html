Teams And Clubs

Learning What Life Needs

Ismailia STEM Astronomy Club

Ismailia STEM Astronomy Club provides a thrilling opportunity for students to experience astronomy phenomenon and explore the wide universe with passion for it, offering an excellent environment for astronomy enthusiast students.

Ismailia STEM Model African Union

Simulating African concerns, Ismailia STEM African Union offers compelling opportunities for students to address and solve Africa’s biggest development challenges through conferences, awareness sessions, and more.

Ismailia STEM Model United Nations

Simulating global issues, Ismailia STEM Model UN helps students engage in diplomacy and critical thinking, organizing awareness events and model conferences to solve world problems collaboratively.

TEDx Youth Ismailia STEM

A non-profit event organization that brings inspiring speakers to students, encouraging them to share ideas in less than 18 minutes across diverse fields.

IYNA Ismailia

The neuroscience club is part of the International Youth Neuroscience Association, promoting awareness and knowledge of the brain and nervous system through creative student-led sessions.

Ismailia STEM Chemistry Club

The Ismailia STEM Chemistry Club aims for your perfection and confidence in understanding concepts, answering problems and freeing your thoughts from traditional chemistry, especially our extracurriculars department which specializes in a plethora of different fields that you might find interesting

Ismailia STEM Music Club

Offering an eccentric opportunity to represent the school in music competitions and activities, this club helps students develop musical talents through training and creative exploration.

Ismailia STEM Mechanics Club

Provides students with training, educational materials, and projects to excel in mechanical fields, preparing them for competitions and future engineering endeavors.

Ismailia STEM Biology Club

A community for students interested in biological science to exchange ideas, explore fun and educational content, and participate in scientific activities.

Ismailia STEM Physics Club

Promotes excellence in both academic and extracurricular physics, offering test banks, summaries, and peer tutoring opportunities for future physicists.

Ismailia STEM electronics Club

Ismailia STEM electronics club indulges the love of software and the ecstatic of hardware in one place gathering circuits and robotics enthusiasms in one community targeting one aim, one electronic community, one love.

Ismailia STEM computer science Club

Ismailia STEM Computer Science club offers deep intellectual level of understanding computer science field, not only that, it helps students compete with such knowledge and utilize it in various life aspectss.

Ismailia STEM English Club

Helps students enhance English language skills academically and practically, providing speaking events and writing workshops to foster confident communicators.
