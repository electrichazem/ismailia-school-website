School History

Our school started its activities in 2015 at a part of a building in the Educational Complex of Ismailia. The school was in its early stages, the official school building wasn't constructed yet—neither the dorm buildings. Students had their meals in the complex restaurant and carried out activities in temporary places. Classes of 2018 never saw the new building, as construction was finished in 2018. Since then, the school has flourished with outstanding students, achievements, and innovation.
